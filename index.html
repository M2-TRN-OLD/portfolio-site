Executive FollowUp

Executive FollowUp is a tool tool to be used by students in upper middle school through college to monitor academic performance. At a glance, a student can see what is due today or this week, as well as, how much study time is needed. With a once a week check-in, the student can keep track of his or her grades and help figure out what he or she wants to study in the future.

shopYourCloset

With shopYourCloset, a user can get and maintain control over his or her wardrobe. You add wardrobe items to the app. The app will, then, compare your wardrobe to the ideal basic wardrobe as described by Real Simple magazine and give recommendations about what items to obtain and what to get rid of. You can choose to donate your unneeded items and the app will create a list for you. Alternatively, you can add your unneeded items to a "Share Closet" to be shared by other users of the app.

getTheBook

getTheBook will help bookclubs choose the right book for the group. You choose a book and the app will pull together reviews, videos and even information about the author and topics discussed in the book.
